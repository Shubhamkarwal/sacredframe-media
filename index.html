About

We don't just photograph retreats.
We witness them.

Sacred Frame Media was born in the Egyptian desert, during a breathwork teacher training that broke open every participant in the room. As the videographer and photographer embedded in that experience, we understood something: the wellness world deserves imagery that matches the depth of its work.

We partner with breathwork facilitators, yoga teachers, retreat organizers, and conscious wellness brands on a monthly retainer basis — becoming your visual storytelling team, not just a one-off hire.

We've documented transformation across continents. We show up, we disappear into the space, and we bring back images that make people feel what happened — without being there.
